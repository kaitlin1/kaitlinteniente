About Me.

Currently serving as the Director of Esports at St. Mary's University. Experienced esports community manager and events coordinator with a history working in the video game and esports industry. Skilled in program management, community management, and esports marketing. Passionate about diversity, inclusion, and lowering the monetary barrier to entry in collegiate esports.
